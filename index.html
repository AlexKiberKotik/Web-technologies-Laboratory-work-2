Royal Deluxe
Royal Deluxe is a collaboration between international recording artist Tyrone Wells and Sam Getz and Jimmy Weaver from the Cleveland-based alt band Welshly Arms. Their sound is aggressive, bad-ass alternative rock tied together with professional instrumentation and craft. In 2015, Royal Deluxe released their self-titled debut EP and unleashed seven high-octane alt songs packed full of retro soul, gritty vocals, and powerful throwback hooks. The trio rapidly amassed a following and now many of Royal Deluxe’s songs have been streamed tens of millions of times. Those numbers skyrocket daily, especially with the latest addition to the Royal Deluxe arsenal. Savages—the third EP from Royal Deluxe released via Position Music in 2019. If the Royal Deluxe EP was an introductory album, then Savages is a sterling achievement for what happens when Wells, Getz, and Weaver turn it up to another level.

Sam Tinnesz
With its mix of rock swagger and pop bombast, Sam Tinnesz's rousing alt-pop found its way into numerous television and film avenues during the late-2010s with credits ranging from major league sports broadcasting to series like Riverdale and commercials for a variety of high-profile retailers. His 2017 debut album yielded the streaming hit "Play with Fire" and kicked off a prolific run of releases over the next few years.
